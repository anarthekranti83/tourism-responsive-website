Top Tourist Places in Pune

Shaniwar Wada

A historic fortification built in 1732 and one of the most famous landmarks of Pune.

Aga Khan Palace

A beautiful palace known for its architecture and role in India's freedom movement.

Sinhagad Fort

A popular trekking destination offering breathtaking views of the surrounding hills.

Dagdusheth Ganpati

One of the most famous and revered Ganesh temples in Pune.

About Pune

Pune is one of the most important cities in Maharashtra, known for its rich history, culture, education, and tourism. It is often called the "Oxford of the East" because of its many educational institutions.

The city is home to famous attractions such as Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, Sinhagad Fort, and many beautiful gardens. Pune attracts tourists from all over India and the world.

Through this website, we aim to provide information about the best tourist destinations, historical places, and travel experiences in Pune.
